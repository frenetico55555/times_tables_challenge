Welcome to the Multiplication Tables Game!

⸻

HOW TO USE:
	•	Open the index.html file in any modern web browser.
	•	Enter your name, click Start, and begin solving multiplication problems (from 1 to 12).
	•	Your performance is tracked: response time, accuracy, and score.
	•	You can reset the timer, take a screenshot, or send feedback (coming soon).
